Northwest Steakhouse Steak Marinade

What is it?

This is a recipe to a marinade (a form of sauce) which accentuates the taste of a delicious cut of steak with Greek garlic undertones.

Ingredients

  • 1 pinch of seasoned salt
  • 1 pinch of garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon of greek seasoning
  • 1 cube of crushed chicken bouillon
  • 1/2 a cup of melted butter

Steps:

  1. Stir all the ingredients into the metled butter
  2. Mix until the bouillon cube dissolves
  3. Pour the marinade into a wide, shallow dish
  4. Place steak into the marinade
  5. Coat both sides
  6. Cook steak to your preference
