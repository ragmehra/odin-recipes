Lemony Chicken

What is it?

This is a recipe to a battered chicken dish with lemon undertones.

Ingredients

  • 1/4th cup olive oil
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 4 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
  • salt to taste
  • 1/4th cup white wine
  • 2 large lemons, juiced
  • 1/4th cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1/4th drained capers
  • 1 large lemon, cut into wedges

Steps:

  1. Heat olive oil in large skillet over medium heat
  2. Place flour in a shallow bowl
  3. Season the chicken breasts with salt
  4. Press chicken into flour to coat
  5. Arrange chicken in the skillet
  6. Cook until golden brown, on each side
  7. Add white wine and lemon juice
  8. Continue cooking until chicken breasts are no longer pink in the center.
  9. Remove chicken breasts into a plate
  10. Stir butter into the remaining sauce until melted
  11. Pour sauce of chicken breasts
  12. Garnish with capers and lemon wedges
