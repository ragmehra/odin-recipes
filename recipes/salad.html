Spring Salad

What is it?

This is a recipe to a salad surprise.

Ingredients

  • 12 slices of bacon
  • 2 heads of broccoli, cut up
  • 1 cup of chopped celery
  • 1/2 a cup of chopped green onions
  • 1 cup of seedless green grapes
  • 1 cup of seedless red grapes
  • 1/2 a cup of slivered almonds
  • 1 cup of mayonnaise
  • 1 tablespoon of white wine vinegar
  • 1/4 cup of white sugar

Steps:

  1. Place bacon in a large, deep skillet. Cook over medium high heat until even brown
  2. Toss together the bacon, brocolli, celery, green onions, green grapes, red grapes and almonds in a salad bowl
  3. Whisk together the mayonnaise, vinegar and sugar. Pour dressing over salad and toss to coat. Refrigerate until ready to serve.
